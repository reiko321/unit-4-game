Who will Rule the Galaxy?

General Grievous

General Grievous is a brilliant Separatist military strategist and a feared Jedi hunter, known for his ruthlessness and hacking cough. His body itself is a weapon, allowing him lightning quick strikes and devastating blows.

Emperor Palpatine

Scheming, powerful, and evil to the core; Darth Sidious was in fact Palpatine, a Senator and phantom menace who slowly manipulated the political system of the Galactic Republic until he was named Supreme Chancellor.

Darth Vader

A former Jedi Knight, Darth Vader was seduced by the dark side by Emperor Palpatine. He remained in service of the Emperor for decades, enforcing his will and seeking to crush the Rebel Alliance.

Darth Maul

A deadly, agile Sith Lord trained by the evil Darth Sidious, Darth Maul is a formidable warrior and scheming mastermind. He wields an intimidating double-bladed lightsaber and fights with a menacing ferocity.
